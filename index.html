stanford women in computer science

The premier student organization at Stanford University dedicated to supporting women in computer science and technology. We build strong communities and professional networks, raise awareness about issues women face in the field and empower women's academic and career success. Join here.

Events

Throughout the school year, WiCS subteams plan a variety of events open to all Stanford students, including coffee chats, panels, workshops, dinners, study nights and more.

Get involved

Subscribe to our mailing list, Google Calendar and Instagram to stay in the loop, or check out our board internship, Fellows and Outreach programs.
